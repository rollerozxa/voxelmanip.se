In 2023, I published a fork of the Luanti engine bundled with NodeCore to Google Play as an experiment in distributing Luanti games independently. It stopped receiving updates after a while, and in February of 2025 it was removed from Google Play along with the rest of my apps.

You are recommended to use the official Luanti Android app available from Google Play, F-Droid or from GitHub, and install NodeCore through the content browser.
