ROllerTest - Outtesting the Minetest.

ROllerozxa's fork of Minetest to implement or merge various new things that currently don't exist in upstream Minetest.

It is the continuation of minetest-rollerozxa, ROllerozxa's collection of miscellaneous patches and personal buildscript for Minetest.

Some new features include Discord RPC, and adding the gamebar and a crosshair to the Android version. Check ROllerozxa's commit history in the ROllerTest repository for a more detailed list of additions.
